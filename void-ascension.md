# Void Ascension

## Vision

A mobile-first 2D Action RPG inspired by the feeling of cultivation
novels. Players explore a seamless world, grow through cultivation,
discover rare Fortuitous Encounters, and admire their own character in a
beautiful 3D Home.

## Candidate Names

-   **Void Ascension** ⭐
-   Path of Dao
-   Realmwalker
-   Boundless Dao
-   Echo of Heaven
-   Beyond Mortality

## Core Pillars

1.  Character Attachment
2.  Visible Progression
3.  Exploration & Fortuitous Encounters
4.  Meaningful Skills with Insight (Intent)

## Home (3D Shrine)

The game opens to a 3D Home where the player's 2D character is rendered
in 3D. Features: - Rotate and zoom the character - Equipment preview -
Aura preview - Weapon display - Pet / Spirit Beast - Story Archive -
Cultivation Realm - Statistics

## World Progression

Players can freely travel between maps.

If a map is too difficult: - Explore another region - Farm equipment -
Seek opportunities - Return stronger

Returning to low-level maps should make the player feel overwhelmingly
powerful.

## Fortuitous Encounters

Rare random events: - Ancient inheritance - Hidden cave - Spirit beast -
Ancient sword - Forgotten memories - Secret cultivation manual

These should feel memorable rather than common loot.

## Insight System

Every skill belongs to an Insight.

Examples: - Sword Intent - Void Intent - Flame Intent - Lightning
Intent - Time Intent - Life Intent

Using skills and exploring the world increases Insight.

At 100%, players awaken a stronger version instead of merely increasing
damage.

## Skill Effect Philosophy

Skills should feel like cultivation techniques rather than MMO spells.

Examples: \### Void Slash Space cracks open and enemies are pulled
toward the fracture.

### Sword Intent

The sword is drawn and nearby leaves, rocks and air itself are cut.

### Heavenly Thunder

Clouds gather, the screen darkens, then a single divine lightning bolt
strikes.

### Time Domain

Time freezes briefly while the player continues moving.

### Mountain Palm

The earth fractures with heavy camera shake.

## Aura by Realm

Foundation: none

Core Formation: faint spiritual energy

Nascent Soul: swirling aura

Void Spirit: distorted space

True Dao: reality bends around the player

## Focus

The player should always feel: - My character has grown. - My realm
matters. - My aura tells my story. - Every map hides another destiny. -
Every breakthrough changes who I am.
