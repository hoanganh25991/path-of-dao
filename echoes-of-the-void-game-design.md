# Echoes of the Void

## High Concept

Mobile-first 2D action RPG with simple controls, collectible heroes,
explorable maps, and a 3D Home.

### Core Pillars

-   One-thumb controls: Move joystick, Attack, Skill, Dodge.
-   Character-first experience.
-   Home screen renders hero in 3D with equipment, stats, lore.
-   Story delivered at the end of each map.
-   Free exploration. If a map is too difficult, travel elsewhere and
    grow stronger.

## Home

The Home is a floating mountain village. Features: - 3D hero viewer -
Equipment - Skills - Bestiary - Story Archive - Map Portal

## RPG Stats

Level, HP, Mana, ATK, DEF, Crit, Crit Damage, Speed, Spirit, Cultivation
Realm.

## Story

Inspired by cultivation fiction. The world contains ten regions. Each
region has 1--2 stages.

### Chapters

1.  Fallen Village --- awaken mysterious jade.
2.  Mist Forest --- rescue a spirit fox.
3.  Stone Canyon --- defeat bandit cultivators.
4.  Moon Lake --- uncover ancient seal.
5.  Burning Desert --- survive sand spirits.
6.  Thunder Peaks --- master lightning step.
7.  Frozen Palace --- meet forgotten queen.
8.  Abyss Rift --- corrupted cultivators.
9.  Heavenly Gate --- challenge guardians.
10. Void Throne --- final confrontation and epilogue.

Each chapter ends with a narrated story scene instead of only rewards.
