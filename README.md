# Path of Dao

Mobile-first cultivation action RPG — explore regions, fight in 2D maps, grow your realm, and return to a 3D Home shrine to admire your journey.

Path of Dao is a mobile cultivation action RPG where you begin as a mortal wanderer and grow through exploration, combat, and quiet perseverance.

FIGHT & EXPLORE
• One-thumb controls — move, attack, dodge, and unleash cultivation skills
• 2D combat maps across multiple regions with bosses and hidden points of interest
• Retreat when a map is too hard, farm elsewhere, and return stronger

CULTIVATE YOUR PATH
• Level up, advance cultivation realms, and unlock Insight awakenings for your skills
• Discover fortuitous encounters — hidden caves, ancient inheritances, and rare rewards
• Earn your legendary blade through exploration, not a tutorial chest

YOUR HOME SHRINE
• Return to a 3D Home to view your character, equipment, aura, and cultivation progress
• Review story chapters, skills, inventory, and choose your next destination on the world map

DESIGNED FOR MOBILE
• Play in your browser as a progressive web app — optimized for phones and tablets
• Save anywhere with local progress; export and import your save file
